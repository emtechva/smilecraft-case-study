01 - Project Context

What This Project Is About

A full patient journey from landing page visit to post-appointment follow-up, built as a connected funnel and CRM automation system.

SmileCraft Dental Clinic is a concept project created to show how a clinic can use GoHighLevel to capture bookings, send reminders, track appointment outcomes, and trigger the correct follow-up automatically.

03 - Automation Workflows

GHL Workflows Behind the System

The backend workflows handle the operational work after a patient books an appointment.

01
Appointment Booked - Confirmation and Reminders
Booking confirmation, 1-day reminder, 4-hour reminder
1
Booking trigger: patient is added to the appointment workflow.
2
Confirmation email: patient receives appointment confirmation.
3
Timed reminders: emails are sent 1 day and 4 hours before the visit.
02
Appointment Status Trigger
Attended, cancelled, or no-show routing
1
Clinic staff receives a status check with trigger links.
2
Clicking a link marks the patient as attended, cancelled, or no-show.
3
The correct follow-up or reschedule branch starts automatically.
03
Cancellation, No-Show, and Reschedule Workflow
Status-based CRM movement and reschedule emails
1
Cancelled path: contact is tagged and sent a reschedule email.
2
No-show path: contact moves to missed appointment follow-up.
3
Attended path: patient receives post-visit thank-you and feedback messaging.
04
Nurture Email Workflow
Longer-term patient follow-up
1
Send the first nurture email.
2
Wait 7 days, then send another follow-up.
3
Wait 30 days, then send the next nurture email.
04 - CRM Operations

Appointment Status Becomes Easy to Track

The opportunity board keeps patient status visible so the clinic can see who booked, who attended, and who needs rescheduling.

CRM Opportunity Board Patients are organized into stages such as New Lead, Appointment Booked, Cancelled/No-Show, and Attended.
Workflow highlight:
After the appointment, the clinic receives a quick status check. One click can trigger the correct attended, cancelled, no-show, follow-up, or reschedule path. This removes guesswork and keeps the next action clear.
